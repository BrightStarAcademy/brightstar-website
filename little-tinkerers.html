🇸🇬 Singapore's FIRST Drama-STEM Programme

Why Choose Little Tinkerers?

Bright Star Academy proudly introduces Singapore's first-ever Drama-STEM enrichment programme, a groundbreaking approach that uses the power of drama, storytelling, and role-play to bring Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) to life for preschoolers.

Designed specifically for children aged 3 to 4, the Little Tinkerers curriculum transforms abstract STEM concepts into exciting, age-appropriate experiences through imaginative play and inquiry-based exploration.

🐝

What Your Child Will Gain

A perfect blend of STEM learning and social-emotional development

🔬

STEM Learning

Our curriculum introduces foundational STEM concepts through engaging, age-appropriate modules. We focus on:

  • Exploring scientific & mathematical principles, like insect life cycles
  • Developing an engineer's mindset through critical thinking and problem-solving
  • Learning tangible concepts like structural stability through real-world applications
❤️

Social Skills

The drama component is fundamental to fostering key social-emotional competencies. Your child will:

  • Build confidence & communication skills through collaborative storytelling
  • Develop empathy by understanding and role-playing a character's emotions
  • Unleash creativity & imagination to bring compelling narratives to life

Our Curriculum

Tailored for how 3-4 year olds learn and make sense of the world

At an early age of 3-4, children are often in the process of constructing their own ideas and framework of the world around them.

Thus, our programmes for this age group mainly revolve around teaching fundamental topics such as "colours" and "shapes" through immersive stories, scaffolded discussions, and intriguing sensory and exploratory learning.

This non-intimidating and fun-filled approach encourages children to be curious and tactile learners as they explore and make sense of the world around them.

Learning Through Play

Our programme is purposefully structured to develop essential skills while sparking curiosity about the world. We focus on:

  • Nurturing observation skills and fine motor development
  • Encouraging communication and collaboration
  • A perfect balance of playful discovery and structured exploration

The 12 Modules

Following the rhythm of the 12-month calendar year, each module corresponds to its number and is thematically linked to a specific topic. Each month consists of 1 module, with 4 lessons approximately 1 hour long.

1
My Reliable Friend

Explore family, friendship, and insect life cycles through Bob the Bumblebee's story.

2
Hands & Feet

Learn rhythm and sound using claps, taps, and stomps inspired by nature.

3
Colours (in the Sky)

Explore sky colours (rainbows, sunrise, night) through stories and movement.

4
Seasons

Learn about the four seasons through drama, music, and themed play.

5
Shapes

Discover and move like 2D shapes through stories and pretend adventures.

6
Emotions

Identify and express emotions like happiness, sadness, and fear through play.

7
Days of the Week

Learn and sequence the days through songs, characters, and movement games.

8
Planets

Explore the planets and their features through movement and role-play.

9
Body Parts

Identify body parts and their functions through games, songs, and drama.

10
Sounds (Insects)

Explore insect sounds and movements through miming, sound play, and sorting.

11
Weather

Learn about weather types and effects through storytelling and physical play.

12
Showcase ⭐

Children revisit key concepts from all modules by preparing a showcase using a chosen presentation format such as performance or installation.
