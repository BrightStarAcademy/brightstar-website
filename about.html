About Bright Star Academy

Born from the hearts of two passionate educators who believe every child deserves to learn through joy, imagination, and purpose.

Our Story

At Bright Star Academy (BSA), we offer a fresh approach to early childhood education that goes beyond traditional methods, using storytelling as a purposeful platform for meaningful STEM learning.

We are passionate about helping young minds fall in love with stories while exploring the wonders of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM).

The concept for Bright Star Academy originates from perceiving education as a space dedicated to creative learning for children. "The Power of Play" helps children learn through hands-on activities in our Innovative Learning Playhouse — a dynamic space where education transcends traditional classroom boundaries.

Here, children engage in creative exploration, developing critical thinking and real-world problem-solving skills.

🌟

Why Drama-STEM, Not STEAM?

While STEAM rightfully acknowledges the importance of the Arts in creative thinking and innovation, it often treats "Art" as a broad umbrella, lacking clear direction or pedagogical focus, especially for early years.

In contrast, Drama-STEM puts a spotlight on one powerful artistic tool: Drama and Theatre.

In our Drama-STEM programme, children are not just passive learners, but explorers, inventors, astronauts, and doctors through role-play, storytelling, crafting, and improvisation.

By embodying scientific concepts and performing STEM ideas, children engage with complex topics in a playful, non-threatening way. As a result, children can truly discover their passion in learning because with Drama-STEM, it's not just facts and figures but stories, adventures, and discoveries.
