About Bright Star Academy

Here at Bright Star Academy (BSA), we aim to provide a new method of education for children beyond conventional education paradigms, one that seamlessly integrates storytelling as a purposeful platform for meaningful STEM learning in early childhood.

At BSA, we are passionate about empowering young minds to not only love stories but also embrace the wonders of Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM).

The heart of how we learn, play, and shine!

Discover the DRAMA-STEM Difference

Unlike STEAM, which treats art as a broad brushstroke, Drama-STEM zooms in on the magic of theatre to bring science to life. Here, children don't just learn—they become what they learn. Whether they're acting out the water cycle or stepping into the shoes of a doctor or atom, young minds explore big ideas through story, role-play, and movement. It's science with feeling. Learning with laughter. And a whole-body adventure that sticks.
