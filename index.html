What is Drama-STEM?

Unlike STEAM, which treats art as a broad brushstroke, Drama-STEM zooms in on the magic of theatre to bring science to life.

Here, children don't just learn—they become what they learn. Whether they're acting out the water cycle, stepping into the shoes of a scientist, or embodying an atom, young minds explore big ideas through story, role-play, and movement.

It's science with feeling. Learning with laughter. A whole-body adventure that sticks.

Our Flagship Programs

Two pathways designed for different developmental stages — each a year-long journey of discovery.

🐝

Little Tinkerers

Ages 3-4

"Discover the power of play!"

At this age, children are constructing their understanding of the world. Our programme explores foundational topics through immersive stories, sensory play, and hands-on exploration — nurturing curious, confident learners who discover joy in learning.

  • 12 themed modules following the calendar year
  • Colours, shapes, seasons, emotions & more
  • Sensory exploration & fine motor development
  • Drama-based storytelling & role-play
Explore Little Tinkerers
🚀

Star Explorers

Ages 5-6

"Inquiry-based learning for growing minds"

Children at 5-6 can spot patterns and grasp abstract concepts. Our programme introduces structured problem-solving through storytelling, crafting, and scientific experiments — building critical thinking and the confidence to form hypotheses.

  • 12 advanced modules with hands-on experiments
  • Light, water cycles, magnets, space & biomimicry
  • Scientific reasoning & hypothesis formation
  • Collaborative projects & presentations
Explore Star Explorers
