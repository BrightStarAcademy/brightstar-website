Our Programs

Two flagship Drama-STEM programmes designed for different developmental stages — each a year-long journey of discovery.

Bright Star Adventures Begin Here

Step into a world where science meets storytelling! At Bright Star Academy, our Drama-STEM programmes are designed to spark imagination, build essential skills, and make every lesson feel like an adventure.

Whether your child is 3 or 6, there's a magical learning journey waiting to begin.

Discover Drama-STEM by Age

🐝

Little Tinkerers

Ages 3-4

"Discover the power of play!"

Introduce your little one to the magic of Drama-STEM — where science comes alive through play, movement, and imagination. At this age, children are constructing their understanding of the world, and our programme nurtures that natural curiosity.

What They'll Learn:
  • Colours, shapes, seasons & emotions
  • Rhythms, sounds & body movements
  • Insect life cycles & weather patterns
  • Days of the week & planets
Skills Developed:
  • Fine motor development & observation
  • Communication & collaboration
  • Confidence through storytelling
  • Empathy & emotional expression

Star Explorers

Ages 5-6

"Inquiry-based learning for growing minds"

Fuel growing minds with hands-on experiments and creative storytelling through our immersive Drama-STEM adventures. At 5-6, children can spot patterns and grasp abstract concepts — our programme challenges them with real scientific inquiry.

What They'll Explore:
  • Light, shadows & colours
  • Water cycles & states of matter
  • Magnets, space & flight
  • Biomimicry & nature's solutions
Skills Developed:
  • Scientific observation & reasoning
  • Hypothesis formation & testing
  • Critical thinking & problem-solving
  • Presentation & teamwork

How It Works

A year-long journey structured around the 12-month calendar

📅

12 Monthly Modules

Each month focuses on one themed module, with 4 lessons (approximately 1 hour each) that build progressively.

📖

Story-Driven Lessons

Every module is anchored by an immersive story that introduces concepts through characters and narratives children love.

🔬

Hands-On Activities

Children don't just hear about science — they do it. Experiments, crafts, and explorations bring learning to life.

🎬

Year-End Showcase

Module 12 culminates in a showcase where children present their learning through performance or creative installation.
