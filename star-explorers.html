Why Choose Star Explorers?

Bright Star Academy proudly introduces Singapore's first-ever Drama-STEM enrichment programme, a groundbreaking approach that uses the power of drama, storytelling, and role-play to bring Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics (STEM) to life for preschoolers.

Designed for children aged 5 to 6, the Star Explorers curriculum integrates STEM with the creative power of drama, preparing children for a confident transition into primary school.

🚀

What Your Child Will Gain

A perfect blend of STEM learning and social-emotional development

🔬

STEM Learning

Our curriculum introduces foundational STEM concepts through engaging, age-appropriate modules. We focus on:

  • Exploring scientific & mathematical phenomena like magnetism
  • Developing an engineer's mindset through critical thinking and problem-solving
  • Learning tangible concepts like structural stability through real-world applications
❤️

Social Skills

The drama component is fundamental to fostering key social-emotional competencies. Your child will:

  • Build confidence & communication skills through collaborative storytelling
  • Develop empathy by understanding and role-playing a character's emotions
  • Unleash creativity & imagination to bring compelling narratives to life

Our Curriculum

Designed for children ready to think like scientists and engineers

At the age of 5-6, children are now more capable to spot and recognise patterns, and can begin to understand abstract concepts.

Thus, our programmes for this age group focuses on introducing them to structured problem-solving activities, where children will learn the theoretical concepts of natural phenomena through storytelling, crafting, and conduct scientific experiments to experience the theory in action.

Inquiry-Based Learning

As children at this age are capable of greater abstract reasoning, our programme introduces them to more complex STEM concepts through inquiry-based learning. They learn to:

  • Ask questions, form hypotheses, test ideas, and reflect on outcomes
  • Take on roles such as scientists and engineers, conducting investigations
  • Direct their own learning instead of passively receiving information

The 12 Modules

This programme builds on children's natural curiosity, introducing scientific concepts through hands-on experiments, storytelling, and role-play. Children develop critical thinking, early scientific reasoning, and the language of science that prepares them for primary school.

1
Scientific Observational Skills

Build scientific thinking by using their senses to explore, observe, and experiment with real-world phenomena like floating and sinking through hands-on and dramatic play.

2
Cycles in Matter - Water

Learn about the 3 states of water and the water cycle through story, science, and play.

3
Light and Shadows

Explore how light forms shadows and experiment with shadow puppetry.

4
Light and Colours

Discover how white light splits into colours and experiment with prisms, filters, and reflection.

5
Light in the Ocean

Explore how light changes underwater and how sea creatures adapt to survive in the deep sea.

6
Diversity of Living Things - Sea Creatures

Transform into sea creatures to learn and present facts through costume, voice, and movement.

7
Magnets and Magnetism

Investigate magnetic forces through experiments and invention-based role play.

8
Space Exploration - Journey to Space

Journey through space to explore planet features, movement, and space science through drama.

9
Number Crunching - Multiplications

Use movement and stories to understand multiplication patterns.

10
How Do Kites Fly?

Explore kite design and the science of flight through hands-on storytelling.

11
Future of Biomimicry: Beyond Imitation

Learn to observe nature's solutions creatively and apply them to solve problems sustainably. Develop curiosity, teamwork, and resilience by exploring and inventing nature-inspired ideas.

12
Showcase ⭐

Children revisit key concepts from all modules by preparing a showcase using a chosen presentation format such as performance or installation.
