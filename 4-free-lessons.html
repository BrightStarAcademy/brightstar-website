Frequently Asked Questions

Is this really free?

Yes! 4 full lessons completely free. No hidden fees, no credit card required. We want you to experience Drama-STEM before making any commitment.

What happens after the 4 lessons?

Nothing, unless you want it to! If your child loves the programme, we'll share our enrolment options. If it's not the right fit, absolutely no pressure.

How do I know which programme is right for my child?

Little Tinkerers is for ages 3-4 (play-based, sensory exploration). Star Explorers is for ages 5-6 (inquiry-based, experiments). Not sure? Just WhatsApp us and we'll help!

Where are the lessons held?

At our centre: 300 Tampines Avenue 5 #07-02, NTUC Income Junction 8, Singapore 529653.
